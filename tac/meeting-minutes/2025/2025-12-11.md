# Announcements
- The [LF Decentralized Trust /dev/weekly developer newsletter](https://lf-hyperledger.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/DR/pages/17170445/dev+weekly+Newsletter) goes out each Friday to hundreds of LF Decentralized Trust developers. It is a collaborative effort. If you have a project release, pull request, community event, and/or relevant article you would like highlighted next week, please [leave a comment for consideration on the upcoming newsletter wiki page](https://lf-hyperledger.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/DR/pages/75268141/2025).
- Maintainer Days registration is now open. Register here: https://register.linuxfoundation.org/event/lfdt-member-summit-2026/register. The Maintainer Days event is free for all attendees and the registration will also include a registration for the LFDT Member Summit happening on January 28-29 if you are a project maintainer. All maintainers attending Maintainer Days are welcome to attend Member Summit even if you are not from a LFDT member company.
- Self nominations for TAC Chair due before 12 DEC 2025. The election for Chair and Vice Chair will run from 12 DEC to 19 DEC.
- [2025 Retrospective](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/154ueDn7MFnGujXhR1GIhTvv0u9Gu_RuS91U6ZfB4ZsI/edit?usp=share_link) available in the December Governing Board update.
- Next week's TAC meeting will be a handover session with the 2026 TAC, where they will be able to get their questions answered from the 2025 TAC.
- December 25, 2025 and January 1, 2026 meetings will be cancelled.
